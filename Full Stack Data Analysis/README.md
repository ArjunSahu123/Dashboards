# 🛒 Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis  
### *Turning Consumer Data into Strategic Business Decisions*

---

## 📌 Project Overview  
From a business perspective, this project focuses on understanding **how and why customers make purchasing decisions**. Rather than just analyzing numbers, the goal is to uncover **behavioral patterns, revenue drivers, and customer segments** that directly impact business performance.

The analysis combines **Python (data preparation), SQL (structured analysis), and Power BI (visual storytelling)** to provide a complete, end-to-end view of customer behavior.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem  
A retail company is experiencing shifts in customer purchasing patterns and wants to answer a critical question:

> **How can customer shopping data be leveraged to improve revenue, engagement, and long-term loyalty?**

Key focus areas include:
- Impact of **discounts and pricing strategies**
- Role of **customer demographics**
- Effect of **subscription and loyalty programs**
- Influence of **shipping preferences and product categories**

---

## 🧠 Business Approach  

As a Business Analyst, the approach followed was:

1. **Understand the Data** → What variables influence customer behavior?  
2. **Structure the Problem** → Convert business questions into analytical queries  
3. **Generate Insights** → Identify patterns that impact revenue and engagement  
4. **Recommend Actions** → Translate insights into business strategies  

---

## 🧰 Tools & Technologies  

- **Python (Pandas)** → Data cleaning & feature engineering  
- **SQL** → Business-driven data analysis  
- **Power BI** → Dashboard & stakeholder communication  

---

## 📂 Dataset Summary  

- ~3,900 customer transactions  
- 18 key attributes including:
  - Demographics (Age, Gender, Location)
  - Purchase behavior (Amount, Category, Frequency)
  - Business drivers (Discounts, Shipping, Subscription)
  - Customer feedback (Review Ratings)

The dataset provides a **complete view of customer journey and decision-making behavior**.

---

## 🔄 Analytical Workflow  

### 🔹 Data Preparation (Python)  
- Cleaned missing values (especially review ratings)  
- Created **customer segments** (New, Returning, Loyal)  
- Built **age groups** for better demographic analysis  
- Standardized data for SQL analysis  

---

### 🔹 Data Analysis (SQL)  
Analysis was driven by real business questions:

- Who contributes more revenue — **male or female customers?**  
- Do **discounts attract high-value customers?**  
- Which products drive **customer satisfaction (ratings)?**  
- Do **subscribers spend more than non-subscribers?**  
- How does **shipping preference affect spending?**  
- What are the **top-performing products per category?**  

---

### 🔹 Dashboard (Power BI)  
An interactive dashboard was built to help stakeholders:

- Track **KPIs (Revenue, Customers, Ratings)**  
- Analyze **customer segments & behavior**  
- Compare **subscription vs non-subscription performance**  
- Identify **top products and categories**  

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights  

### 📊 Customer Segmentation  
- **50% New Customers** → High conversion opportunity  
- **35% Returning Customers** → Growth potential  
- **15% Loyal Customers** → High-value segment  

👉 *Focus should be on moving customers across this funnel.*

---

### 💰 Revenue Drivers  
- **Female customers generate slightly higher revenue**  
- **Express shipping users spend ~12% more**  
- **High-value discount users exist** → discounts don’t always reduce value  

---

### 🛍 Product Insights  
- **Clothing category dominates revenue**  
- **Top-rated products (Blouse, Dress)** show strong customer satisfaction  
- Some products are **heavily discount-dependent**  

---

### 🔁 Subscription Behavior  
- Subscribers show **higher engagement and repeat purchases**  
- However, non-subscribers still contribute significant revenue  

👉 *Subscription model needs optimization for better value delivery.*

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations  

### 1. Improve Subscription Strategy  
- Introduce **exclusive benefits**  
- Offer **personalized rewards**  
- Increase perceived value  

---

### 2. Target High-Value Segments  
Focus on:
- Young adults  
- Female customers  
- Express shipping users  
- Smart discount users  

---

### 3. Optimize Discount Strategy  
- Avoid blanket discounts  
- Target **price-sensitive segments/products**  
- Protect profit margins  

---

### 4. Strengthen Customer Journey  
- Convert **New → Returning → Loyal**  
- Introduce **tiered loyalty programs**  
- Personalize engagement  

---

### 5. Leverage Product Insights  
- Promote **top-rated products**  
- Use **bundling strategies**  
- Expand high-performing categories
